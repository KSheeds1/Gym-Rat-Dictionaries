Welcome to Gym Rat Dictionaries

Your one stop shop for exercise definitions!

Have you ever came across an exercise and not known how to perform the movement? Confused by terms such as Barbell Good Mornings or Dumbbell External Rotations? Well look no further! GRD is a dictionary of exercise terms curated by our wonderful users! Browse through our most recently added definitions over on definitions or search for a specific definition using our search feature!

GRD is a community built site, with gym rats of all fitness levels coming together to contribute a variety of exercise definitions to help cut through the jargon associated with exercise. Our purpose is to provide a site that will be beneficial to those at any level of fitness! For those who are just starting off and struggling with the terminology, to those who are well versed in the fitness world and want to impart their knowledge to others.
